Bio

Sydney is in her last year at the University of Virginia studying Environmental Science and Global Culture and Commerce. She is very involved in the entrepreneurial community at UVa and is especially passionate about social entrepreneurship. She loves playing soccer, going to the beach, and travelling.

Why I think coding is important

"I've visited a few startups and one thing they all have in common is their need for programmers. It is becoming such a crucial thing to all businesses, and I think that everyone who wants to be in the business community should gain some experience in this field!"

Why I'm excited for iExperience

"iExperience is an amazing and unique opportunity for people to learn how to code and experience the incredible life and culture of South Africa. I recently traveled to South Africa and fell in love with it, and I want as many people as possible to be able to have this experience as well!"
