What is the program?

iExperience is an 8 week, full-time summer program, designed to turn you into a web developer - A 4 week intensive coding bootcamp, followed by a 4 week hands-on internship at a local tech company. The goal is to give you the equivalent skillset and experience of an entry-level Ruby developer. That means after the program, you will be equipped with all the tools needed to build web applications. You don't need any prior web experience to be able to attend, just a do-it-yourself attitude and desire to have a summer experience of a lifetime.

The Curriculum

The best way to learn is by doing. We will have you coding, and coding… and coding!

HTML and CSS

Core fundamental skills to the world of web development which you will master by the end of this program.

Javascript and JQuery

Bring the web to life with jQuery-powered interactions and an understanding of the core of JavaScript.

Ruby on Rails

Learn one of the best ways to build modern, database-backed web applications

Databases

Relational or Document Store? MySQL or Postgres? Mongo or Redis? Cloud or dev/null? Learning the fundamentals to data storage is a must. And yes, you will know SQL.

Agile Development

Agile is more than just a development practice, it's a philosophy for life. Develop and iterate on your projects by keeping a close eye on costs and value.

Product Management

Product management lies at the intersection between business, design, and technology; giving you the foresight to gauge whether a product is viable from a business perspective.

Git - Source Control

Manage your code versions, and collaborate on projects with other programmers.

Work Experience

Nothing prepares you for the real world quite like the real world! Spending time coding is where you will gain the real skills. Your one-month internship will give you exactly that.

7 AM

9 AM
