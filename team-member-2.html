Darren Kramer has been a professional poker player for the last 10 years. In the last 6 years he has been actively involved in becoming a full time venture capitalist. He has founded 2 companies: a thriving conference company - livingyourbrand.com and an online property portal - propertytube.com.

Darren has a huge passion for education and South Africa, which lead to him starting his own charity, bottomup.biz, with two amazing people, Ashley and Helene Visagie. Bottom Up began as a small project and has recently grown immensely. Its aim is to make young kids' school experience much richer by focusing mainly on literacy, physical education and the value of acquiring skills.
